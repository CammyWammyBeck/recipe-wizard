Delete Your Recipe Wizard Account

Last updated: April 20, 2026

You can permanently delete your Recipe Wizard account and all associated personal data at any time. This page explains how to make that request and what happens afterward.

1. How to Request Deletion

Send an email from the address associated with your Recipe Wizard account to the address below with the subject line Delete My Account.

Email a deletion request

Or copy this address manually: privacy@cameronbeck.dev

Please send your request from the email address you used to sign up. We use the sending address to verify the request so no one else can delete your account.

2. What Data Will Be Deleted

When we process your request, the following is permanently deleted from our servers:

  • Your account profile (name, email, hashed password)
  • All recipes you have generated, saved, or modified
  • Your shopping lists and ingredient preferences
  • Your dietary restrictions and cooking preferences
  • Your recipe prompt history

3. What Data May Be Retained

A small amount of data may be retained for legal, accounting, or security reasons:

  • Purchase records: Subscription and transaction records held by Apple App Store or Google Play are retained by those providers according to their own policies. We do not control or delete those records.
  • Anonymized logs: Server logs that no longer contain personal identifiers may be retained for up to 90 days for security and diagnostic purposes.

4. Response Time

We will confirm receipt of your request within 3 business days and complete deletion within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email at the address we deleted.

5. Canceling a Subscription First

Deleting your account does not cancel any active subscription. To avoid further billing, please cancel your Recipe Wizard Premium subscription through your device's subscription settings before submitting a deletion request:

  • Google Play: Open the Play Store → Menu → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions.
  • Apple App Store: Open Settings → your name → Subscriptions.

6. Questions

If you have any questions about the deletion process or our handling of your data, please contact us at privacy@cameronbeck.dev.

This page describes the account deletion process for Recipe Wizard as required by Google Play and Apple App Store policy. For our full privacy policy, see privacy-policy.html.
