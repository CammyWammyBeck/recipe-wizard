Privacy Policy for Recipe Wizard

Last updated: April 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Recipe Wizard ("we," "us," or "the app") handles information when you use the Recipe Wizard mobile application. We keep this policy short and specific: it only describes what the app actually does.

1. Information We Collect

Recipe Wizard collects only the information needed to run the service:

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and password when you create an account. Passwords are stored only as a salted hash; we never see or retain the plaintext.
  • Recipe content you create: The prompts you submit, the recipes generated for you, your saved recipes, your shopping list, and your cooking preferences (servings, difficulty, dietary restrictions).
  • Subscription information: If you purchase Recipe Wizard Premium, the app store (Apple or Google) processes the payment and RevenueCat records whether your subscription is active. We do not see or store your payment card details.
  • Basic device metadata via RevenueCat: When you interact with the subscription system, RevenueCat collects standard device information (device model, OS version, app version, anonymous install identifier) in order to deliver entitlements correctly.

The app does not collect location data, contacts, photos, microphone or camera data, or analytics/usage telemetry. No third-party analytics SDK is embedded.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate recipes based on the prompts you submit.
  • To maintain your account, preferences, shopping list, and recipe history so they persist across sessions and devices.
  • To verify your Premium subscription status and deliver the features it unlocks.
  • To respond to you if you contact support or privacy at privacy@cameronbeck.dev.

We do not use your recipe prompts or generated content to train any AI model, and we do not sell your data to anyone.

3. Third-Party Services

We share the minimum data necessary with the following processors, each acting on our behalf:

  • OpenAI: Each recipe prompt you submit is sent to the OpenAI API so a large language model can generate the recipe. OpenAI's API terms state that data submitted via the API is not used to train their models. Your prompt is sent without your name or email attached.
  • RevenueCat: Manages subscription state, entitlements, and purchase restoration.
  • Hosting and database providers (currently Heroku): Host our backend API and store your account, recipes, and shopping list data.
  • Apple App Store and Google Play: Process subscription payments. Their own privacy policies govern that data.

We may also disclose information when required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

4. Data Security

  • All traffic between the app and our backend is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS).
  • Passwords are stored only as salted hashes.
  • Access tokens are stored on your device using the operating system's secure storage.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account and recipe data for as long as your account exists. Server logs that no longer contain personal identifiers may be retained for up to 90 days for security and diagnostic purposes. Backups are rotated on a rolling basis and any remaining copy of deleted data is removed within 35 days.

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Update your name, email, or password from inside the app.
  • Request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data by following the instructions at Delete Your Account. Deletion is completed within 30 days of request.
  • Email privacy@cameronbeck.dev with any other privacy question or request.

7. Children's Privacy

Recipe Wizard is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us at privacy@cameronbeck.dev and we will delete it.

8. Subscription Management

Recipe Wizard Premium is billed through your Apple or Google account. You can view, change, or cancel your subscription in your device's subscription settings. Deleting your Recipe Wizard account does not automatically cancel your subscription; please cancel it first.

9. International Users

Recipe Wizard is operated from Australia. If you use the app from another country, you consent to your information being processed in Australia and in any country where our processors (listed in section 3) operate.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will change whenever we do. Material changes will be highlighted in the app.

11. Contact
